Pollymorph is a collaborated project between Casper Liu, Allen Chen and Suzin You for COMP 206 Assignments at McGill University.
(Instructor: J. Vybihal)
The goal of the project is to develop an easy to use survey-making system with a clean visual appeal.
Users can add questions to their own survey and distribute the survey to his/her target group, and the survey result will be presented in a table.
With Pollymorph, creating a survey has never been this quick and easy! Sign up now for free!
